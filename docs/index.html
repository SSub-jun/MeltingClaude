What you get

An animated icon in your menu bar transitions through 4 stages as your usage climbs toward the 5-hour rate limit.

Click the icon to see your current 5-hour block, today's totals, recent messages, and a 7-day usage chart.

Pick Pro, Max 5×, Max 20×, or Custom — the app adjusts color tiers to your subscription's typical caps.

Reads your local Claude Code session logs only. No servers, no API keys, no telemetry. The app makes zero network requests.
